How to play

Use the left and right arrow key to move the ninja cat left and right.
Catch the chicken nuggets to score 50 points and gain a speed up power.
Catch the donuts to score 20 points, but each one slows you down.
Catch the fish to score 10 points.
Avoid the hot peppers, they make you lose 75 points and instantly freeze you.
Avoid the water drops, they cost a life. You have 9 lives.
